Kromatika always requires a fixed fee. This fixed fee is the gas cost of the trade processor (ChainLink Keepers) and it is dependent on the current gas fee. Users can also choose to specify the gas price at which they want their trades to be executed. This in turn can delay the trade execution a bit but it is an option for lowering this fixed service fee. The user always pays this fee in the form of $KROM tokens.
